Iiro Jäppinen

I design user interfaces, icons and logos for the digital world. Lately I’ve been defining user stories and turning them into valuable experiences. I make projects happen.

At the moment I work at Verkkokauppa.com, Finland’s largest and best-known online retailer. My goal is to create the best shopping experience for all kinds of people. My customers include our workers and creating tools for every step of the online sale — from the Add to Cart button to warehouse transfer tools — is what I do daily.

Before that I helped create the Humble Indie Bundles by designing their brand and early promotional pages. I also got to be on-site in San Francisco making this happen. I’ve also helped various people create their products, mostly by designing icons and UI.
